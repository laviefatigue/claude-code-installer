The Flow of Creation

You type a simple phrase into the terminal. Not a command, but a wish. "Build me a landing page," you say. The intent is captured, not just the syntax.
Claude Code looks at your directory, the context of your digital soil. It understands the ecosystem. It drafts a plan, weaving together files, functions, and styles like threads in a tapestry.
With your permission, it acts. Files appear. Dependencies resolve. The structure rises from nothing. You are the architect; it is the builder.
You converse. "Make it warmer," you say. "Fix the edge case." The cycle continues, a dialogue between human creativity and machine precision, until the work is done.
