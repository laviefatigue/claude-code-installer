# Opening a Project

Claude Code understands your project by looking at the files in your folder.

## Why Open a Folder?

When you open a folder, Claude can:
- **See all your files** — and help you edit them
- **Understand your project structure** — knowing what goes where
- **Create new files** — in the right places
- **Run commands** — from your project's directory

## Where to Start?

**Option 1: Create a new folder**
Make a folder called `my-first-project` on your Desktop or Documents.

**Option 2: Open an existing project**
Have code you're working on? Open that folder.

**Option 3: Download a project**
Ask Claude to help you clone something from GitHub.

## Tip

You can always change folders later. Just go to **File → Open Folder** or press `Ctrl+K Ctrl+O`.
