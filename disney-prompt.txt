You are creating a GROUNDBREAKING browser experience - not a webpage, but a DISNEY ANIMATED FILM told through scroll and cursor interaction.

THE VISION:
Imagine the opening of Aladdin - the merchant, the lamp, the magic unfolding. Now translate that to browser. The scroll IS the story. The cursor IS the viewer's hand. Every pixel scroll reveals more magic.

CORE MECHANIC - THE LAMP & THE SMOKE:
- Page opens with a SINGLE golden lamp centered on deep midnight background
- As user scrolls DOWN, luminous blue/purple SMOKE begins pouring from the lamp
- The smoke is CSS/canvas animated - swirling, organic, alive
- The smoke FILLS the viewport as scroll progresses
- Within the smoke, colors emerge: sunset oranges, magentas, golds
- Each color band contains a STORY CARD that fades in - revealing what gets installed

SCROLL MECHANICS:

CURSOR INTERACTION:
- Cursor near the lamp causes it to glow brighter
